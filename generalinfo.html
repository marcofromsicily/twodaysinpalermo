General info


Transports, events, food and cool stuff

Transports


  • Palermo Airport is 30 km from the city center, buses run every 30 minutes on each direction.
  • Urban transports options are available on the Moovit App.

Events


  • Palermo is the 2018 Italian Capital of Culture.
  • Palermo will guest the European Nomadic Biennial "Manifesta" in 2018.

Food


  • Antica Ficacceria San Francesco is a traditional Sicilian food restaurant.
  • Fud is a modern Sicilian food restaurant.
  • San Lorenzo Mercato is an urban market with many Sicilian food restautants.
