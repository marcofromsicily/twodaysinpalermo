Palermo city center


Unesco world heritage



Suggested walk path.

Cattedrale


Palermo Cathedral is an Arab-Norman church dedicated to the Assumption of the Virgin Mary.

The Cathedral is an Unesco World Heritage.

Piazza Bologni


Piazza Bologni is a central square with bar and reastaurants.

Contemporary art museum "Riso" is locared in the square.

San Giuseppe dei Teatini


San Giuseppe is a breathtaking baroque church.

Quattro Canti


Piazza Vigliena is a square with four baroque buildings.

Fontana Pretoria


Fontana Pretoria is the city monumental symbol.

San Cataldo and Martorana


The two churches of San Cataldo and La Martorana are
an Arab-Norman Unesco World Heritage.

Teatro Massimo


Teatro Massimo is the biggest opera house in Italy.

Teatro Politeama


Teatro Politeama is a neoclassical building and the house of Orchestra Sinfonica Siciliana.
